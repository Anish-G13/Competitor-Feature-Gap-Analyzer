📊 Competitor Feature Gap Analyzer
An AI-driven strategic benchmarking tool for automated competitive intelligence and roadmap prioritization.

🎯 Executive Summary
This application was engineered to transform the manual, time-intensive process of competitive benchmarking into a real-time, data-driven workflow. By leveraging Gemini 1.5 Flash, the tool identifies critical product features within a category, benchmarks competitors, and provides weighted gap analysis to inform product strategy and M&A due diligence.

🚀 Key Features
Automated Feature Discovery: Dynamically identifies the top 5 critical features for any product category using LLM-based market intelligence.

Dynamic Weighted Scoring: Allows Product Managers to assign "Importance Weights" (1-10) to features, recalculating market coverage in real-time.

Strategic Visualizations: * Radar (Spider) Charts: Visualizes the "Strategic Shape" and feature overlap between products.

Weighted Bar Charts: Quantifies the competitive gap using custom business priorities.

Consulting-Ready Insights: Generates "Executive-Style" commentary (Strengths, Neutral Observations, and Critical Risks) formatted for senior leadership review.

Exportable Data: One-click CSV export for integration into slide decks and strategic reports.

🛠️ Technical Stack
Frontend: Streamlit (Python-based web framework)

AI Engine: Google Gemini 1.5 Flash via Google Generative AI SDK

Data Processing: Pandas for weighted score calculations and data transformation

Visualization: Plotly for interactive Radar and Bar charts

📈 Use Cases
Product Management: Identify "must-have" vs. "differentiator" features to prioritize the engineering roadmap.

Management Consulting: Rapidly generate competitive landscapes and market parity reports for client deliverables.

Sales Enablement: Empower sales teams with a technical "Cheat Sheet" comparing product capabilities against specific competitors.
